pseudorandom.js
PseudoRandom provides a mechanism for generating deterministic psuedo random numbers based on a seed. Based on the Park-Miller algorithm. See http://www.cit.gu.edu.au/~anthony/info/C/RandomNumbers and http://www.erikoest.dk/rng.htm.
